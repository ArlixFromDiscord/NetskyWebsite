About NC Network

What is NC Network?

NC Network is a Discord Server with technology and anime in mind.

Founded on July 14th, 2017 after a failed attempt at merging Crashie's Lair and Netsky Network, NC Network stood for Netsky-Crashie Network.

After the sepration it stayed as NC. It was re-merged on October 11th, 2017. NC Network has grown slowly to 100+ members.

NC Network is the hub for all things relating to The Crashie Brand, and AeroFusion. The Crashie Partners also reside there.
